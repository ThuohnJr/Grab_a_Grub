Cockroach feeds

Cockroaches, the mere mention can give you chills. This insect can cause mayhem in probably every African home. If they don't bother you we advise to take a DNA test to trace your origins. You may be asian.Despite their look and body size, cockroaches are one of the most resilient species on earth known to survive even nuclear bombings. This quality makes them a very reliable livestock.Another welcoming quality is its ability to convert waste food into protein mass. Therefore instead of buying very expesive feeds, you can just feed them directly on waste food from the community and get good quality livestock. We urge you to buy our feeds that are 100% natural and are very, very, veery nutritious.

We have a selection of species. Our top choice is the american cockroach(Periplaneta americana)
